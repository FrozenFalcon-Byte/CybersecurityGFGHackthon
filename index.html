Password Strength

In the realm of online security, a strong password system is paramount, acting as the frontline defense against unauthorized access. Password checker plays a crucial role in this defense by evaluating factors such as length and character diversity.

Strength Checker

A Strong Password Must Contain:

  • At Least 8 characters length
  • At least 1 number (0...9)
  • At Least 1 uppercase letter (a...z)
  • At Least 1 special character (!...$)
  • At Least 1 uppercase letter (A...Z)
